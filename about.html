About Us

Welcome to the Mega Church of Picnic Goers! We are a vibrant community dedicated to spreading joy and love through our unique approach to worship. Our church is more than just a place of worship; it's a place where everyone is welcome to bring their favorite picnic items and enjoy a day of fellowship and fun.

Our services are designed to be inclusive and engaging, with a focus on community and connection. Whether you're a seasoned picnic enthusiast or new to the world of outdoor worship, you'll find a warm and welcoming atmosphere at the Mega Church of Picnic Goers.

Join us for our next service and experience the magic of picnic worship for yourself. We can't wait to see you there!
